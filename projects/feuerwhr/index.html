Unsere-Feuerwehr

Unsere Feuerwehr or “Our Fireman” is a Nomik project that is responsobile for firefighters getting fonds for new equipment. By purchesing a mobile phone 10% of all purcheses go to the firefighter comunity. Nomik will be providing this offer in all regions of Austria. They needed to simplify their website using CMS to simply update the mobile phones for sale. We proceded to create a visual idnentity, websites, posters, S.M. posts, banners and flyers, etc. for them.

Client: Nomik (Cimet)
Role: Visual Identity, UX/UI Design, Graphic Design
Date: 2019
