BIOGRAPHY

Hello, I am Aleksandar Stojanović, a digital designer focused on digital product and brand identity while keeping an approach that is minimalistic with clarity and balance in mind.

I find it easy to work with developers, present my concepts, and build design systems that are well-thought-out and simple to implement. I am confident in my work and do not hesitate to freely exchange ideas to ensure productive collaboration. Always excited to work with passionate people and shape technology into experiences that are memorable and express the idea clearly.

Pixel perfection is never a goal, it’s a rule.


AGENCIES

I've been lucky enough to have worked and collaborated with global brands including T Mobile, Magenta, A1, Nomik, HAAI.

TOOLS

Sketch, Figma, Illustrator, Photoshop, HTML/CSS and Cinema 4D.

Depending on the project scope, I prototype with different tools, such as Principle, InVision, ProtoPie, and Adobe XD.

CAPABILITY

Research, Art Direction, Visual Identity, Web Direction, UI Design, UX Design, Graphic Design, Layout and typography, Wireframing, Low and high fidelity prototyping, Interaction and motion design.
